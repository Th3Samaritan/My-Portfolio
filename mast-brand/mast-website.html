Six letters.
One geometry.

The MAST prism is not merely a symbol — it is a cipher. Every letter of the company's name, and every initial of the founder's full name, is geometrically encoded within the same unmodified prism. Nothing was added. They were always there.

M in the outer frame and twin peaks. A in the top face. S in the diagonal arc. T in the vertical spine. C in five outer edges. O in the full perimeter. The mark signs itself completely.

M
Inner Frame · Twin Peaks
Outer left and right legs, peak slopes, and center valley. Muyideen · Materials · Manufacturing
A
Top Face Geometry
The entire top face is the letter A — apex, two slopes, crossbar. AbdulSamad · Advanced
S
Diagonal Arc · Pivot
Upper-right arc to center, pivot, lower-left arc to base. Science · Software · Systems
T
Crossbar · Full Spine
Horizontal crossbar at the top level, full vertical spine apex to base. Teniola · Technology
C
Outer Hex · Left Arc Open
Five of six outer edges. The boundary that holds everything. Consolidate
O
Full Outer Perimeter
The complete closed hexagonal boundary. The ring that contains all three worlds. Oladimeji

How We Work

Methodology of
consolidation.

I

Analyse

We map the full problem space across domains. Where others see isolated engineering challenges, we see interconnected systems waiting to be optimised together.

II

Simulate

Computational models first — FEA, CFD, ML-driven property prediction, digital twins. We test in simulation before committing to material reality.

III

Fabricate

Additive and subtractive manufacturing guided by data. The algorithm decides the geometry. The printer builds it. The material validates the model.

IV

Deploy

Software, hardware, and intelligence delivered as integrated systems. Built to operate, not just demonstrate. Tested to certification standards.
