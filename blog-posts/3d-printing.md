# 3d printing
At Some point during the design ep I had to do some 3d printing of some servo cover an electrical component that controls the aileron or something am too sure about the function, around the wings area for flight, and we needed to cover that, so I had to design the servo cover and also 3d print it, material used for printing was pla, so I spent the entire night printing, infact before the actual printed output there was scaling issues, not until I found right scale for the material, then it was successfully printed, and the 3d printer model was Ender I think version 3.
